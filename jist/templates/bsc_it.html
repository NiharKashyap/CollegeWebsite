About Department

The Computer Science and IT department was introduced in the year 2007, with a three year professional course in B.Sc. (IT) affiliated to Dibrugarh University with an aim to expand the Institute and as well impart professional education to the students of the locality. The course is the most upto-date one. The department also impart courses in other B.Sc. and Engineering Departments. Almost, all out going students are either employed in many reputated MNCs and in Govt. Institutions/Govt. Undertakings, besides doing higher studies.
